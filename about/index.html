About

Hi all, I’m a software engineer currently working on search infrastructure at Apple in Zurich. Before that, I was at Amazon’s Berlin office.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree from the University of Trento and a Master’s degree from the University of Milan. During my studies, I developed a strong interest in algorithms, data structures, distributed systems, databases, and search engines. I explored these areas through my Master’s thesis on graph compression and through side projects, including implementations of an LSM tree and a search engine.

I had the chance to compete in SWERC, a team-based competitive programming contest, in both 2020 and 2022. I also enjoyed participating in Google Hash Code whenever I could.

Outside of work, I spend time practicing calisthenics and working on my cooking skills. I read occasionally, though probably not as much as I should.
